Heart Failure Prediction¶

By: Savi R Bhide

Preamble:¶

Cardiovascular diseases is one of those diseases which causes most of the deaths.
Some of the major contributing factors of the heart failure are anemia, daibetes, high blood pressure, smoking, etc. All these factors when goes beyond their safe limit causes cardiovascular diseases.

This project has all these attributes, so let's have a look how different factors effects heart failure.
